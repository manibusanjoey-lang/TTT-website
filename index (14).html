Meet the Founder

Joey Manibusan

Army Veteran · Cybersecurity Expert · Entrepreneur · Explorer

TTT wasn't born in a boardroom — it was born on the road, in the gym, and inside the mind of someone who refused to sit still. As a U.S. Army Veteran, I had the rare privilege of seeing the world while serving — living in South Korea for a year and Germany for three years, experiencing cultures most people only read about. Those years didn't just build discipline. They built perspective.

I've always trained hard, traveled far, and pushed myself to grow intellectually — whether that's diving deep into cybersecurity, building businesses from the ground up, or simply reading one more book than yesterday. TTT is the brand I built for every person who lives that same hunger — the ones who train not just for aesthetics, but for performance and longevity. The ones who travel not as tourists, but as students of the world. The ones who transform — not in bursts, but every single day.

The world is your oyster. You just have to explore it.

Joey is also a published author. His book, "Where Structure Ends, Growth Begins", is a deep exploration of what happens when you strip away the rules, the routines, and the expectations — and finally give yourself permission to evolve beyond them.

"The most dangerous person in the room is the one who never stops growing — who trains their body like a weapon, travels the world like a student, and transforms their mind like it's the only battlefield that truly matters."

— Joey Manibusan, Founder of TTT
